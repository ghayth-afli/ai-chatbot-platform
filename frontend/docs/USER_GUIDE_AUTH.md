## Getting Started

To access Nexus Chatbot, you need an account. You can create one in two ways:

- **Email & Password**: Traditional account creation
- **Google Account**: Quick sign-in using your Google account

### Visit the Login Page

1. Go to [https://nexus-chat.ai](https://nexus-chat.ai)
2. Click **"Sign Up"** to create an account
3. Or click **"Log In"** if you already have an account

---

## Creating an Account

### Step 1: Fill Out the Signup Form

1. Click **"Sign Up"** on the main page
2. Enter your information:
   - **Email**: Your valid email address
   - **Password**: A strong password (at least 8 characters with uppercase, lowercase, number, special character)
   - **Confirm Password**: Re-enter your password to confirm
3. Review the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
4. Click **"Sign Up"**

### Password Requirements

Your password must contain:

- ✓ At least 8 characters
- ✓ One uppercase letter (A-Z)
- ✓ One lowercase letter (a-z)
- ✓ One number (0-9)
- ✓ One special character (!@#$%^&\*)

❌ Passwords are **not allowed** to:

- Contain your email address
- Be a common password
- Be previously used (system remembers last 5 passwords)

### Example Passwords ✓

```
SecurePass123!
MyP@ssw0rd2024
ChatBot#Security9
```

### Step 2: Wait for Confirmation

After signing up, you'll see a notification: **"Check your email for verification code"**

If you don't see this message, check:

- Your spam folder
- Confirm your email address was spelled correctly
- Wait 1-2 minutes for email delivery

## Email Verification

### Why Verify Your Email?

Email verification ensures:

- Your email is real and currently active
- You can recover your account if locked
- We can send important security notifications
- Compliance with spam regulations

### Verify Your Email

1. **Check your inbox** for email from noreply@nexus-chat.ai
2. **Look for a message** titled: "Verify Your Email Address"
3. **Click the verification link** in the email OR
4. **Enter the code**: If there's a code, enter it in the verification dialog
5. **Done!** Your account is now verified

### Verification Code Validity

- ⏱️ Codes expire after **24 hours**
- 🔄 Request a new code: Click "Resend Code" button
- ·👍 No limit on code requests

### How Long Does Verification Take?

Verification links should work immediately:

- **Instant**: Clicking the link
- **Automatic**: System checks after 30 seconds if you don't click
- **Manual**: Enter code at any time within 24 hours

### Language Options

- 🇺🇸 English version selected automatically
- 🇸🇦 Arabic (العربية) version also available
- ⚙️ Check email preferences to change language

## Logging In

### Steps to Log In

1. Go to [https://nexus-chat.ai/login](https://nexus-chat.ai/login)
2. Enter your **email address**
3. Enter your **password**
4. Click **"Log In"**
5. You'll be redirected to your **Chat Dashboard**

### Cookie Consent

First-time login will ask: **"This site uses secure cookies for authentication"**

- ✓ Click **"Accept"** to proceed
- These cookies are necessary for the service to work
- Read our Privacy Policy for details

### Remember Me Option

Check **"Keep me logged in"** to:

- ✓ Stay logged in for 7 days
- ✓ Skip login on future visits
- ✗ **Not recommended** on shared computers

### Account Verification Status

If your email isn't verified:

- ⚠️ You may see a warning: "Please verify your email"
- ✓ You can still access basic features
- 🔽 Some features may be limited

**Solution**: Complete email verification (see [Email Verification](#email-verification))

### Login Rate Limiting

For security, failed login attempts are limited:

- 📛 **5 attempts** per 15 minutes
- 🔒 Account locked temporarily if limit exceeded
- ⏰ Wait 15 minutes before trying again
- 📧 Security email sent if suspicious activity detected

## Forgot Password

If you forget your password, don't worry!

### Steps to Reset Password

1. Go to **Login Page**: [https://nexus-chat.ai/login](https://nexus-chat.ai/login)
2. Click **"Forgot Password?"** link
3. Enter your **email address**
4. Click **"Send Reset Link"**
5. **Check your email** for reset link
6. **Click the link** in email (valid for 1 hour)
7. **Enter new password** (follows same requirements as signup)
8. **Confirm new password**
9. Click **"Reset Password"**
10. **Log in** with your new password

### Password Reset Email

- 📧 Check spam folder if email doesn't arrive
- ⏱️ Links expire after **1 hour**
- 🔄 Request new link: Click "Forgot Password?" again
- 🌍 Email sent in your preferred language (EN/AR)

### Important Security Notes

- 🔒 **Never share** password reset links
- ✅ **Verify sender**: Only emails from noreply@nexus-chat.ai are legitimate
- ⚠️ **Suspicious emails**: Report suspicious password reset emails
- 🤝 **Support won't ask**: We'll never ask for your password

## Google Sign-In

### What is Google Sign-In?

Google Sign-In lets you:

- ✓ Create account using your Google credentials
- ✓ Log in instantly without remembering another password
- ✓ Link existing account to Google
- ✓ Merge accounts if you had both email and Google login

### First-Time Google Sign-In

1. Click **"Sign in with Google"** button
2. Select your **Google account**
3. Review permissions (we access email only)
4. Click **"Continue"** or **"Allow"**
5. You're logged in! 🎉

### If You Already Have an Account

**Case 1**: You have an email account + want Google login

- Log in with email
- Go to Settings → Account
- Click "Link Google Account"
- Follow Google authentication flow
- Now you can use either method

**Case 2**: You have both email and Google accounts

- System detects this on second Google login
- Offers to **merge accounts**
- Your chat history stays with merged account
- You can now log in with either method

### Security with Google

- 🔐 Your password is never shared with Nexus
- 🔒 Google manages authentication securely
- ✅ Your email is verified automatically
- 🛡️ Additional security through Google's 2FA if enabled

### Unlinking Google Account

If you want to remove Google linking:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Account**
2. Scroll to "Connected Accounts"
3. Click **"Unlink Google"**
4. Confirm by entering your email password
5. Google account is now unlinked

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: "Invalid Email or Password"

**Cause**: Email or password is incorrect

**Solutions**:

1. ✓ Check **email spelling** - no spaces
2. ✓ Check **CAPS LOCK** not on
3. ✓ Verify **password** character by character
4. ✓ Use **"Forgot Password"** to reset
5. ✓ Try signing up if account doesn't exist

### Problem: "Account Locked - Too Many Login Attempts"

**Cause**: 5+ failed login attempts in 15 minutes

**Solutions**:

1. ⏰ **Wait 15 minutes**, then try again
2. 📧 **Reset password** via "Forgot Password"
3. 🔄 Try a **different email** if this is a new account
4. 💬 Contact support if problem persists

### Problem: "Email Verification Code Expired"

**Cause**: Code older than 24 hours

**Solutions**:

1. 🔄 Click **"Resend Code"** button
2. 📧 Check email for **new code**
3. ⏰ Use new code within 24 hours
4. 💬 Contact support if email not received

### Problem: "Can't Receive Verification Email"

**Cause**: Email delivery issue

**Solutions**:

1. ✓ Check **spam/junk folder**
2. ✓ Verify **email address** spelling
3. ✓ Try **"Resend Code"** button
4. ✓ Wait **1-2 minutes** for delivery
5. ✓ Add **noreply@nexus-chat.ai** to contacts
6. 💬 Contact support with your email

### Problem: "Google Sign-In Not Working"

**Cause**: Browser settings or Google account issues

**Solutions**:

1. 🔄 **Refresh page** and try again
2. 🍪 Clear **browser cookies** and try again
3. ✓ Verify **Google account is active**
4. 🔓 Ensure **pop-ups not blocked**
5. 🌐 Try **different browser** or private mode
6. 💬 Contact support if persists

### Problem: "Password Requirements Not Met"

**Cause**: Password doesn't meet security requirements

**Solutions**:

1. ✓ Add **uppercase letter** (A-Z)
2. ✓ Add **lowercase letter** (a-z)
3. ✓ Add **number** (0-9)
4. ✓ Add **special character** (!@#$%^&\*)
5. ✓ Make sure **8+ characters** total
6. ✓ Remove **your email address** from password

### Problem: "This Email Is Already Registered"

**Cause**: Account already exists with this email

**Solutions**:

1. 📧 Use **"Forgot Password"** to recover account
2. 🔗 Or use **"Log In"** instead of "Sign Up"
3. 🔄 If Google sign-in available, use that
4. 💬 Contact support to merge accounts

## Privacy & Security

### Your Data is Safe

We protect your information with:

- 🔐 **256-bit encryption** (HTTPS) for all connections
- 🛡️ **Hashed passwords** - we never store plain passwords
- 🔒 **HTTP-only cookies** - inaccessible to JavaScript
- ✅ **Security headers** - protects against common attacks

### What We Collect

**Required for Service**:

- ✓ Email address
- ✓ Password (hashed)
- ✓ Language preference
- ✓ Usage statistics (anonymized)

**NOT Collected**:

- ✗ Payment information (free service)
- ✗ Phone number (unless you provide)
- ✗ Location data
- ✗ Device identifiers

### Your Rights

You can:

- 📥 **Download** all your data as JSON
- 🗑️ **Delete** your account and all data
- ✏️ **Modify** your profile information
- 🔐 **Change** your password anytime
- 🚫 **Opt-out** of analytics

### Security Best Practices

1. ✓ Use a **strong, unique password**
2. ✓ Enable **Google 2FA** on your Google account
3. ✓ Don't share **login credentials**
4. ✓ Don't use **public WiFi** for sensitive actions
5. ✓ Log out on **shared computers**
6. ✓ Check **"Suspicious Activity"** alerts
7. ✓ Review **"Active Sessions"** regularly
8. ✓ Update **password quarterly**

### Reporting Security Issues

Found a security vulnerability?

- 🚨 **Don't create public issue**
- 📧 **Email**: security@nexus-chat.ai
- Provide **detailed description**
- Include **steps to reproduce**

We acknowledge all security reports within 48 hours.

## FAQ

### Q: Can I have multiple accounts?

**A**: No, email addresses must be unique. But you can link multiple login methods (email + Google) to one account.

### Q: How long am I logged in?

**A**: 7 days if you check "Keep me logged in", otherwise 15 minutes of inactivity.

### Q: Can I change my email address?

**A**: Yes, in Settings → Account → Email. You'll need to reverify.

### Q: What if I delete my account?

**A**: All data is permanently deleted after 30 days. You can cancel within 30 days.

### Q: Is my chat history private?

**A**: Yes, it's encrypted end-to-end. Only you can see it.

### Q: Can I export my data?

**A**: Yes, go to Settings → Data → Download. You get a JSON file.
